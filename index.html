Our Story

Discover Our Passion

Founded in 2010, Café began as a small family-owned coffee shop with a vision to bring authentic Indian coffee culture to the modern world.

Our name "Desi" comes from the traditional 84-bean coffee blend that our founder's grandfather used to make in his village, using a secret combination of spices and brewing techniques.

Today, we continue this legacy by carefully selecting the finest coffee beans and ingredients, combining traditional recipes with modern preparation methods to create unique flavors that tell our story.

Every cup we serve carries the warmth of our heritage and the passion of our team.
